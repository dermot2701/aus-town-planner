How a review is grounded
  • Retrieval is scoped to the proposal's municipality (LPS) plus statewide SPP.
  • Every finding cites a supplied clause ID or a TASCAT citation — nothing is invented.
  • Where context is insufficient, the review says so and lists what is needed.
  • Output is a single structured JSON object, assessed Acceptable Solution first, then Performance Criterion.
